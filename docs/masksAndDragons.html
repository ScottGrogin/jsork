Nine Blades Masks and Dragons

This report will generate a list of players who have a Mask or Dragon award and the respective level. If the column next to the level of award is TRUE, then the result was computed by counting the number of that type of award and not the Rank specified in the awards given.

You can copy the results to the clipboard once the report is complete or use the browser print option.
