Park Herald Report

This report is intended to provide a summary of Park information. It could be used during a lull between games or before your Althings. Currently, it looks at Officers, Last attendance taken, Reeve Qualified, Knights, Paragons, Players who should be waivered, Players About to Level, Players Recently Leveled, Park Birthdays, Marked Active but not attending, and optionally Active but marked Inactive report. If there's something you think is missing that is in another report or could be generated from ORK Data please send that suggestion to Kismet

WARNING: Do not run this on a cell data plan since it may consume significant bandwith.
