Kingdom Titles

!! Experimental !! This report will show all the active players with titles and those without within a Kingdom/Principality. If you don't see your title, perhaps look at your ORK page and discuss it with your Chancellor/PM.

Once the results are shown there will be a Copy to clipboard option that you can use to get the contents and paste into a spreadsheet for manipulation. For example, only show those with titles.

WARNING: This report will take a while and use data, don't run it on your cell phone data plan! WARNING!!
