Spring Muster Kingdom Averages

This report will calculate new members in a Kingdom during a particular year for Spring Muster. The dates used are March 1st thru May 31st inclusive. Each new player during that time means 10 points. Each new player who also signs in at least 6 times is worth 75 points. Other point calculations will be done via the organizing committee.

WARNING: This report will take a while and use data, don't run it on your cell phone data plan! WARNING!!
