Viridian Outlands Voting

!! Experimental !! This report will generate a list of players within a Viridian Outlands park that can vote. A player must have attended 6 different days in the last 6 months anywhere in the Viridian Outlands and be dues paid. The Viridian Outlands Week starts on Monday and ends on Sunday. A signed waiver is not a requirement but is listed to show the waiver status.
If a player is Not marked as a valid voter, see the highlighted red areas for why.


The whole list is provided here and you can use the CSV export to copy the results to a spreadsheet.
