Family Trees

A family tree in Amtgard includes those that have been Paged, Squired, Woman-at-Arms, or Man-at-Arms to another player. The ORK only shows your titles but does not show who you may have given these titles to. In addition, many of these titles were given before the current ORK and only show up as "Historical Imports". These are not included in the results shown in this tool. If you search a name below and either the search shows "There's no family tree info" or after you click on a name and the resulting tree does NOT look complete, you may have to talk to a local Prime Minister or a Kingdom/Principality Prime Minister about updating an ORK profile to have the correct family tree information.

The information used for this tool is aquired by running an extensive query once in a while and saving the results. It is not computed live from ORK data. So if you have updated yours or anothers ORK profile to have the correct family tree information, it may take a while to show up. Ping kismet [at] felfrost.ca if you'd like it regenerated on demand.

Search for a player and see if that player has a family tree. Click on the name if they do and navigate by clicking on the various individuals names. The tree will redraw accordingly. Remember you can use abbreviations in the search like "nb: kismet" to only search in the Nine Blades or "kop:mtd hammer" to only look in Mystic Dawn.
