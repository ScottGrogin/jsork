Kingdom Ladder Candidates

The intention of this document is to keep a record of who has met, or is near meeting, the minimum qualifications for knighthood as described in the Amtgard Rules of Play. This document relies on award entries in the ORK. If an entry doesn't seem accurate, check the player on the ORK. Each player has a link to the ORK proper. By default the report only includes players who have reached level 7 or higher.

Crown: A player may be eligible for Knight of the Crown after obtaining Masterhood in the Crown.
Flame: A player may be eligible for Knight of the Flame after obtaining Masterhood in Rose, Smith, or Lion.
Serpent: A player may be eligible for Knight of the Serpent after obtaining Masterhood in Owl, Dragon, or Garber.
Sword: A player may be eligible for Knight of the Sword after obtaining Masterhood of the Order of the Warrior, known as a Warlord.

You can print the results from the browser after the query has been completed.
You can copy each individual ladder to the clipboard for pasting into a spreadsheet.
