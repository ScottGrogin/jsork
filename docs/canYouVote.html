Can You Vote?

You can search for a player and when you click/select that player their voting status will be shown and rewrite the URL so you can bookmark the results. When you go back to that saved page it will bypass the search and just show the player's voting status. The idea is to limit the use of the FULL voting report by players and thus limit the impacts on the ORK itself. Remember you can use kingdom and park abbreviations to help find a player (eg: "nb:ff kismet"). Not all Kingdoms that have a jsork voting report are supported yet, I'm adding them one at a time. I'm at 2 out of 11.
